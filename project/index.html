Mikis The Great: Dedicated to Mikis Theodorakis

Welcome to the web page dedicated to the world famous composer and politival activist Mikis Theodorakis whose life, music and ideals became synonymous with modern Greece. The purpose of this page is to introduce this incredible personality of music and freedom to the entire world.
